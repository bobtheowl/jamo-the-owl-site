Jamo the Owl Learns to Fly

Jamo the Owl has a big day ahead of him. One of the most important days in any bird life: it's time to learn to fly! On his first attempt, Jamo learns an important lesson about perseverance and not giving up.

This children's book teaches youngsters that even if you don't succeed at first, when you keep trying you can achieve your goals.
